How to get your bank statement

You need a PDF statement to run your audit. Here's exactly where to find it in OPay, Moniepoint, and GTBank — no branch visit required.

OPay Moniepoint GTBank
OP

OPay

From the OPay app — takes about a minute.

1

Open the OPay app and tap Wallet on the home screen.

2

Tap the Transaction History icon at the top right.

3

Tap the ⋯ or Statement option, then choose your date range.

4

Tap Download or Export as PDF. OPay emails it to you or saves it to your phone's downloads.

📄 OPay statements list each transaction with two dates (transaction date and value date) — The Audit's parser already handles this format.
MP

Moniepoint

From the Moniepoint MFB app.

1

Open the Moniepoint app and tap More in the bottom navigation.

2

Select Account Statement.

3

Pick a date range (last 30, 90 days, or custom) and tap Generate.

4

Tap Download PDF or check your registered email for the statement.

Tip: If it lands in your email instead of downloads, save the attachment to your phone before uploading.
GT

GTBank

Via the GTWorld app, or USSD/internet banking as a backup.

1

Open GTWorld and log in, then tap Accounts.

2

Select your account, then tap Statement Request.

3

Choose a date range and tap Request. It's emailed to your registered address as a PDF.

4

No app access? Visit Internet Banking on desktop, or call *737*6*1# for a mini-statement (note: USSD won't give a full PDF).

📄 GTBank PDFs sometimes need a password (often your account number or a PIN sent by SMS) — have that ready before uploading.
