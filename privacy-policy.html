Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Overview

The Audit is a browser-based financial document scanner. All document processing happens locally on your device. No data is uploaded to any server at any time.

2. Data We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data or financial information. PDFs you upload are processed entirely in your browser and never leave your device.

3. Cookies & Advertising

The Audit uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may use cookies to show relevant ads based on your browsing activity. You can manage or opt out of ad personalization at Google Ad Settings .

4. GDPR EU · UK · Switzerland

We do not collect personal data. If you are located in the European Economic Area, United Kingdom, or Switzerland, a consent banner will appear to help you manage Google's ad preferences in compliance with GDPR.

5. CCPA California

We do not sell your personal information. To opt out of Google's ad personalization, visit Google Ad Settings .

6. Third-Party Services

The Audit uses PDF.js (Mozilla) to process documents locally in your browser. No document content is sent to Mozilla or any third party.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
