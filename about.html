What is The Audit

The Audit is a free, browser-based financial document scanner that helps you understand your spending at a glance. Upload your bank statement PDF and The Audit instantly breaks down your income, expenses, transfers, and gives you a personal finance score — all without leaving your browser.

Why We Built It

Most people have no idea where their money goes every month. Bank apps show you transactions but rarely show you the full picture. The Audit was built to change that — to give anyone a clear, honest snapshot of their financial health in seconds, for free, with no signup required.

How It Works

Everything runs locally inside your browser. Your PDF never leaves your device — it is never uploaded to any server, stored, or shared. Here is what happens when you upload a statement:

📄

PDF Scanning

Your statement is read directly in the browser using PDF.js by Mozilla.

🔍

Smart Detection

Income, credits, debits and expenses are automatically identified and totalled.

📊

Visual Breakdown

See your spending split into categories with a clear visual score circle.

🔒

100% Private

No data leaves your device. Not even your file name is sent anywhere.

Who It Is For

  • Anyone who wants a quick overview of their monthly finances without using a full budgeting app
  • Freelancers and self-employed individuals tracking income and outgoings
  • Students learning to manage money for the first time
  • Anyone who values privacy and does not want to hand their bank data to a third-party service
